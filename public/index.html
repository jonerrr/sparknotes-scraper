Measure For Measure

Act 1 Scene 1

5

Enter DEMETRIUS and PHILO

PHILO

Nay, but this dotage of our general’s

O’erflows the measure. Those his goodly eyes,

That o’er the files and musters of the war

Have glowed like plated Mars, now bend, now turn

The office and devotion of their view

DEMETRIUS and PHILO enter.

PHILO

No, our general’s infatuation is out of control. His eyes used to glow with pride when he reviewed his troops. Now his eyes themselves exclusively to a certain brown-skinned face. His heart used to burst the buckles on his breastplate in great fights, but now he’s lost all temperance and dedicates his heart to satisfying the lust of an Egyptian whore.
